Quick answer

The Anker PowerCore 20,000mAh Portable Charger is the most well-rounded high-capacity power bank for most people, balancing reliability, speed, and portability.

If you need the most capacity for your dollar, grab the RAVPower 30,000mAh unit; for fast laptop charging on a budget, the Baseus 30,000mAh with 65W PD is the value champ; and for the fastest recharging of both your devices and the bank itself, the Anker 737 (PowerCore 24K) leads the pack.

A high-capacity power bank, one with 20,000mAh or more, is the difference between a dead phone mid-trip and staying connected for days. These bricks can charge a smartphone three to five times, top off a tablet, or even juice up a laptop in a pinch. But more capacity usually means more weight and slower recharging, so the right pick depends on how you travel and what you charge. When choosing, look past just the milliamp-hours. Check the power delivery (PD) wattage for fast charging, the number of ports, and the overall size. Also consider how fast the bank itself recharges, some 30,000mAh models can take over eight hours with a standard charger.
